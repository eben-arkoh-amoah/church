OUR MISSION

As one of the churches of Christ, our core mission is preach the gospel of Christ our lord and saviour to all souls and make the manifold wisdom of God known to all municipalities and powers on earth. By so doing, make it known to all within our reachin every medium that Jesus is the Christ whom we need to blieve, confess to, be baptised unto his name and serve him our Lord and savior!
