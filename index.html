OUR MISSION

As one of the churches of Christ, our core mission is to preach the gospel of Christ, our Lord and Savior, to all souls and proclaim the manifold wisdom of God to every community and authority on earth. By doing so, we aim to make it known to all, through every available medium, that Jesus is the Christ. He is the one we must believe in, confess to, be baptized into, and serve as our Lord and Savior!
